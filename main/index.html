Shrawya Nayana 3.0

Welcome to Shrawya Nayana, an initiative by Rotaract Club of CINEC dedicated to bringing the joy of storytelling to visually impaired children. We believe that every child deserves to experience the magic of stories, and through our audiobook project, we aim to make this possible.


Through Shrawya Nayana, we aspire to promote literacy, imagination, and joy through sound.
