Our Mission

We provide service to others, promote integrity, and advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through our fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders.

OUR STORY

The Rotaract Club of CINEC Campus is built upon the hard work of many individuals striving together as one towards a common goal. The club which was chartered in 2018 has followed a steady upward trajectory with its performance. Completing five amazing years of service, the club has now embarked on its sixth successive year as an institution-based volunteer service club.


In addition to being a group of responsible and conscious youth, has undertaken numerous charitable and socially impactful projects for the betterment of the community and society. We have members from all departments and faculties and currently have an active membership of over 300 members.


The Rotaract Club of CINEC Campus holds prestige over many achievements including being awarded as the 'Emerging Club of the Year' in year 2020/2021 among many other accolades and was able to secure Rotary Citations for the years 2020/21, 2021/2022, and 2022/2023. The club has completed numerous key projects that have positively impacted the community

What is SHRAWYA NAYANA 3.0 ?


Shrawya Nayana 3.0 is the third consecutive edition of our signature project by the Rotaract Club of CINEC Campus, focused on supporting individuals with hearing and vision impairments. In Phase 1, we visited the Sri Chandrasekara Deaf School in Moratuwa, where we conducted an art and origami workshop, donated essential supplies, and helped set up a learning aid room. The project aims to empower differently-abled individuals through creativity, education, and sustainable support.


What happens in Phase 2?

In Phase 2 of Shrawya Nayana 3.0 we focus on creating and donating audiobooks for visually impaired individuals. These audiobooks support both educational and recreational needs, making learning more accessible and engaging. The phase promotes inclusive education and provides long-term resources to empower their academic journey.

Our Objectives
  • Empower the hearing and visually impaired through education and creativity.

  • Provide accessible resources that make a lasting difference.

  • Promote inclusivity and raise awareness in the community.
